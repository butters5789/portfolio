Pick-A-Resort

This app contacts the Vail EpicMix API to grab weather and snow data from Keystone, Breckenridge, Vail and Beaver Creek ski resorts. By using the day selector, you can compare the snow conditions and forecasts across the four resorts.

Tech: Node.js, Express, SCSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, AJAX
